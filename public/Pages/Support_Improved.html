Frequently Asked Questions

How does my donation help?

Your donation helps us maintain the platform, improve features, and run community awareness campaigns. With BloodBridge, donations are connected to lifesaving impacts - on average, one donation can save up to 3 lives.

Is my donation tax-deductible?

BloodBridge is currently a community initiative. We're working towards official NGO registration to provide tax benefits. Contact us for more information on our registration status.

Can I donate blood even if I'm not in Ghana?

Currently, BloodBridge operates in Ghana. However, you can support our mission through financial donations, volunteering remotely, or promoting the platform in your network.

What are donor eligibility requirements?

Blood donors must be at least 17 years old, weigh 110+ pounds, in good health, and have a valid ID. Some medical conditions may prevent donation. Visit your nearest blood bank for a detailed health screening.
