Privacy Policy

Protecting your data, securing your trust

Trust Us

What Information We Collect

BloodBridge collects personal details such as names, contact information, addresses, and blood types to ensure effective and reliable donor-recipient matching.

How We Use Your Information

  1. Facilitating urgent donor-recipient connections efficiently.
  2. Enhancing platform functionality using anonymized analytics.
  3. Providing updates, reminders, and system reliability alerts.

How We Protect Your Data

BloodBridge adopts state-of-the-art security measures including encryption, secure servers, and regular audits. Our team prioritizes the integrity and confidentiality of all user information.

Your Privacy Rights

You have the right to:

  1. Access the personal information we hold about you.
  2. Request updates or corrections to inaccurate details.
  3. Ask us to delete your personal information at any time.

Need assistance? Contact us at: support@bloodbridge.com

User Consent & Policy Updates

By using BloodBridge, you agree to our privacy policy, and we ensure full transparency in data usage. Any updates will be communicated via email and posted on our website.

Transparency & Our Commitment

BloodBridge is built on trust. We do not sell or disclose personal information to third parties without explicit consent, except when required by law. Our mission is to uphold the highest standards of privacy protection.
